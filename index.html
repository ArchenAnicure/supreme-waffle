Controls

Hands

Hold Neck + R View pulse on a body
RMB + LMB Perform CPR on a body
E + LMB Loot/search a body
LMB / R Raise/lower fists

Ragdoll Mode

RMB Right hand
LMB Left hand
ALT Grab with right hand
SHIFT Grab with left hand
W / S Move forward/backward
A / D Roll left/right
E Stabilize position (allows proper weapon use)
fake Exit ragdoll mode

Weapons

Hold R Check magazine
E (while aiming) Switch sights (multi-sight scopes)
SCROLL Change zoom (variable scopes)
R + LMB Change melee mode (knives, fire extinguisher, etc.)

Miscellaneous

C + E Enable night vision goggles
Q Show interaction menu
F1 Spawn menu (sandbox mode)
fake + shift + e Exit seat/vehicle
G Drop items
Hold E (in seat) Go numb

Medical Guide

Using Medical Items

LMB Use medical items on yourself
RMB Use medical items on others

Medical Items

Bandage – Heals bleeding
Beta-Blocker – Lowers adrenaline levels
Big Bandage – Heals bleeding with larger capacity
Bloodbag – Blood transfusion (hold R to switch between modes; sometimes spawns empty)
Decompression Needle – Heals collapsed lungs from gunshot wounds
Epipen – Increases adrenaline, may restart the heart
Fentanyl – Strong opioid (~300% pain reduction). Use in small doses; risk of overdose (use Naloxone). Useful for dislocations
Mannitol – Heals head trauma from blunt force or gunshot wounds
Painkillers – Mild opioid (~35% pain reduction). Helps with fixing dislocations
Tranexamic Acid – Heals internal bleeding (MEDKIT EXCLUSIVE)
Tourniquet – Reduces blood flow to a limb, minimizing bleeding
Morphine – Opioid (~100% pain reduction). May wake someone from a coma (reduces unconsciousness)
Naloxone – Reduces opioid levels. Useful for overdoses (e.g., from fentanyl)

Medkit Contents

  • Bandages
  • Painkillers
  • Tranexamic Acid
  • Tourniquet
  • Decompression Needle

Symptoms & Treatment

White Static – Internal damage, permanent (e.g., shot in the lung, fractured pelvis) or blood loss (use a Bloodbag)
Red Static – Pain. Use an opioid (in case of overdose, use Naloxone) or wait for it to subside
Black Vignette – Shock resulting from pain, blood loss, or head trauma. Can lead to unconsciousness (use Morphine to minimize coma time; be cautious of overdosing the patient). Use Mannitol for head trauma
Unable to Get Up – Broken spine (paralyzed) or broken legs. Pay attention to on-screen notifications (use medkit bandages to heal limbs; spine damage is permanent)
Colorful, Pulsating Screen – Overdose. Use Naloxone immediately
Low Pulse – Failing heart. Use adrenaline (Epipen)
High Pulse (While Standing Still) – Imminent cardiac arrest. Use Beta-Blockers
No Pulse – Perform CPR. Use adrenaline for extended pulse time
Not Breathing – Suffocation from various causes: underwater, overdose (use Naloxone), lungs shot (use Decompression Needle). Perform CPR
